Project Title :A solar panel cleaning robot for residential and commercial installed sites

 

motivation : The solar PV modules are generally employed in dusty environments, the dust gets accumulated on the front surface of the module and blocks the incident light from the sun. It reduces the power generation capacity of the module. The power output reduces as much as by 10 to 30 % if the module is not cleaned for a month and use of solar panel in residential sector is increasing day by day which generate requirement of efficient cleaning system as life of solar panels are generally from 20-25 years

 

sketch of final project

Features of robot

Water free :

Water free cleaning system will work with harsh climatic condition and avoid cost of water

Effective :

Regular cleaning of panel will increase annual energy production capacity by 10 % to 25 % use of microfiber brush to

Remove dust will not damage the panels

Autonomous :

Clean the solar panel  by the set time automatically and automatically reduce operation and maintenance cost ,it also have option to control manually

Flexibility

It can be use for varieties of solar panel and mounting types including fixed tilt array and multi array structure of solar roof top
